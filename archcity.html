About the Project

During the Summer of 2019, I had a Graphic Design Internship with ArchCity Defenders, a legal non-profit that provides holistic legal advocacy and represents people who are targeted by the criminal-legal system.

As the Graphic Design Intern, I created numerous posts for social media and designed content for use on the web and print. I also created an image series in Photoshop of several clients for use in Anniversary marketing.

Skills

  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
  • After Effects

Digital Paintings

The largest project during my internship was creating four digital paintings that were used in the 10th Anniversary marketing materials (brochures, website).
